For my first project at General Assembly, two weeks into the program, I recreated this French puzzle online.

In addition to basic HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JQuery, I taught myself about SessionStorage and incorporated it into the app. The user inputs how many tiles they'd like to play with and the app retrieves records for play with the respective number of tiles.

I'm still very proud of this project, not for any complexity of code, but for the problem solving and creative solutions I was able to find this early in the program.
