+One (Plus One) is an app I developed with Hannah Schuster and Nyketha Lyken for our Group Project through General Assembly. The app helps individuals link up with others for events and activities they don't want to attend alone.

With an Angular front-end and a Rails API, this single-page application allows you to sort activities by predefined cateogries or your own unique search query.
