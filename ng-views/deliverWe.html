DeliverWe was the second project that I developed at General Aseembly. Built entirely on Rails, this app allows users to better arrange and manage large delivery orders with many people.

This app allows users to search for and join planned delivery orders in their area, splitting tip and mitigating delivery fees. Alternatively, users can suggest an order of their own and wait for others to join them.

A detailed and accurate receipt is produced, explaining each individual's bill, including tax and portions of the delivery fee and tip. No more will you have to argue or debate who owes what. This app takes care of that for you.
