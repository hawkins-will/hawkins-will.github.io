About Section

Hi there! I'm Will Hawkins, a professional Actor and Singer turned full-stack Web Developer.

In April 2017, I completed the Web Devbelopment Intensive at General Assembly. For fourteen weeks, I studied full-stack development and found myself thrilled by the challenges I was presented with! I view each project I work on as a puzzle, and I use my artistic background to come up with innovative and creative solutions.

After studying Classical Music and Dramatic Art at UNC - Chapel Hill I moved back to the DC area and have been active in the local Theatre and Music scenes since. I've been fortunate enough to perform at many fantastic venues including The Kennedy Center, The Lincoln Center, and in The Legend of Zelda Symphonic Concert at The Warner Theatre. I'll never leave this part of me behind, but I'm thrilled with the new directions my life is taking me.
